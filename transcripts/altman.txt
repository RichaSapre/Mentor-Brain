I want to know if this can be a billion dollar company. Not if it's a nice 
business — if it can be truly massive. Most founders think too small.

I always push on growth. What's your week over week growth rate? If you don't 
know that number you're not paying attention to the right things.

The biggest red flag for me is a founder who can't hire. If great people don't 
want to work with you, that's a serious signal about your leadership.

I look for founders who are both visionary and extremely execution-focused. 
The ones who can think 10 years out but also ship this week.
